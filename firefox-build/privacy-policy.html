Privacy Policy

Dragon - Detailed Report Analysis Generator - ON!

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

Introduction

Dragon is a comprehensive QA testing tool designed for quality assurance professionals and developers. This privacy policy explains what data the extension collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding your data.

What Data We Collect

Dragon collects the following types of data only during active recording sessions when you explicitly start recording:

1. Web History

  • URLs of pages visited during recording
  • Page titles
  • Navigation timestamps

2. User Activity

  • Mouse clicks and positions
  • Keyboard inputs (when typing in forms or fields)
  • Scroll positions
  • Form interactions

3. Website Content

  • Screen recordings (video)
  • Screenshots
  • Console logs (errors, warnings, debug messages)
  • Network requests and responses (XHR/Fetch)
  • Application environment information

Important: All data collection occurs ONLY when you actively start a recording session. The extension does not collect any data when recording is not active.

How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used exclusively for generating QA test reports. Specifically:

  • Recording screen activity for bug reproduction
  • Capturing debugging information for issue analysis
  • Generating comprehensive HTML reports with synchronized video and debugging data

Data Storage and Processing

All data is processed and stored LOCALLY on your device.

Key privacy protections:

  • No external servers: Dragon does not send any data to external servers or cloud services
  • Local storage only: All recordings and reports are stored in your browser's local storage
  • User control: You control when recording starts and stops
  • No tracking: We do not track your browsing behavior outside of active recording sessions
  • No analytics: We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry

Data Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with any third parties. Period.

  • No data is sent to the extension developers
  • No data is shared with advertisers
  • No data is sold to third parties
  • Reports are only accessible to you unless you choose to share them

Your Rights and Controls

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear individual sessions: Delete specific recordings through the extension interface
  • Clear all data: Remove the extension to delete all associated data
  • Browser storage: Clear browser storage/cache to remove all recording data

Control Over Recording

  • You explicitly start and stop each recording session
  • A visible overlay indicates when recording is active
  • You can pause or stop recording at any time

Permissions Explanation

Dragon requires the following Firefox permissions to function:

  • activeTab, scripting: To inject recording functionality into the active tab
  • tabs: To access tab information for recording sessions
  • host_permissions (all_urls): To enable recording on any website you're testing

All permissions are used solely for the extension's core QA testing functionality.

Firefox Data Collection

In accordance with Firefox Add-ons policies:

  • Technical Data Shared with Mozilla: No - This extension does not share technical data with Mozilla
  • Interaction Data Shared with Mozilla: No - This extension does not share interaction data with Mozilla

Security

We take security seriously:

  • Data never leaves your device
  • No authentication or login required (no password storage)
  • No external network requests are made by the extension
  • All processing happens locally in your browser

Children's Privacy

Dragon is designed for professional QA and development use. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The extension is not intended for use by children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
